I am an associate professor in School of Computer Science, Xi'an Jiaotong University.

I received my Ph.D from CGVU group, School of Informatics, University of Edinburgh, under the supervision of Taku Komura. My PhD was supported by the CSC–Edinburgh Joint Scholarship. I received my M.Sc from School of Computer Science and Engineering, Southeast University.

Research interests:

Shape Analysis, Geometry Processing, Interaction Analysis
