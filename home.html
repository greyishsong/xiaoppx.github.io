Xi Zhao (赵玺)

I am a lecturer in the department of computer science, Xi'an Jiaotong University.

I received my Ph.D from CGVU group, School of Informatics, Edinburgh University, under the supervision of Taku Komura. My PhD was supported by the CSC–Edinburgh joint Scholarship. I received my M.Sc from School of Computer Science and Engineering, Southeast University. My research interests include shape analysis, geometry processing and interaction analysis.
